Products

We are working on products that can bring a massive change in the way people are treated in all kinds of Health Centres

State Tracker

A state of the art analytics tool that can help doctors keep track of a state of a patient in long term. This state could var from sugar level for a Diabetic patient, Weight of a cancer patient or state of mind for a psychiatric patient allowing doctors and caretakers to provide better care for the patient.

Virtual Clinic for Psychologists

This tool can help psychiatrists and psychologists be in contact with their patients even off session allowing them to monitor their progress improving communication with patients and allowing them to be decision makers

Dashboard for Long term Care Centres/Rehabs/Psychiatric Centres

This tool can help the overworked and understaffed employees to track better care of their patients and also help the families be more involved in the treatment as a patient moves along a disease trajectory or transitions from one care setting to another
